SHD India is a volunteer driven event. Thanks to our doners and community supporters, we are able to keep the ticket prices as low as possible. Our aim is to keep SHD accessible to every science enthusiast. If you are excited to attend SHD and finding it difficult to afford the ticket, please reach out to us at shdbelgaum[AT]gmail.com We will help you with reduced cost or a free ticket.
